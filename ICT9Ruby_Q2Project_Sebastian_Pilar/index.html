The Philippines is a beautiful place, and I am lucky to be born and be currently living here. Because our country is an archipelago, there are a lot of beaches in the area. Last year, I was lucky to visit three of them. They may not be the best places in the whole country, but they are definitely places that would be worth your time, especially if you like the sea. Allow me to list things that I personally took note of when visiting these regions.

Baler

  • lots of walking distance places to go to besides the beach
  • high waves and high sun
  • fine and light sand, full of crabs
  • beginner surfer friendly environment

Zambales

  • wide beach with clear water
  • fine and light sand
  • strong waves and strong currents

Pagudpud

  • very scenic route going to and from here
  • crashing waves and strong wind
  • stony and rough sand

Sources:

My own experience when visiting these regions and Trip Advisor (not a sponsor, hehe).
